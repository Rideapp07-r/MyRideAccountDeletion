Users can request the deletion of their MyRide account and all associated personal data at any time.

How to request deletion

Include the following information:

What happens next

  • Your request will be reviewed
  • Your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted
  • This process may take up to 7 business days

Data that will be deleted

  • Account information (email, profile data)
  • Ride history
  • Location data
  • Payment references (excluding data retained by Stripe for legal obligations)

Some data may be retained temporarily if required by law or for fraud prevention.
